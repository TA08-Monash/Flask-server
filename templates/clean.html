Trust

Bought in Oct '21. If your TV doesn't have a Disney+ app available, this is a *great* way to get around that - better than using a laptop or some other computer. The volume control doesn't work with our '21 model Hisense 4K TV, but I can live with it. I have never bothered with the gimmick that is voice recognition: navigating menus is fine enough. The remote is super small and frankly, cheap + flimsy. I like the ability to download apps, and it's nice that it has an app to view Twitch (even if interacting with chat is painful, it's fine if you just want something different on in the background). A big bummer is that it needs its own power (and the power form the TV's USB ports won't work), but you'd need to power a laptop etc anyway. Occasionally, the remote won't respond for a couple of seconds, so the device can feel unresponsive. Overall however, worth a buy - especially if you can get a discount.
